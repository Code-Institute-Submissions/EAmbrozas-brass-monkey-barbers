THE RIGHT PLACE FOR THE RIGHT GENTLEMAN

With an old school flavor and new school twist, Brass Monkey Barber Shop specializes in classic cuts, beard trims and hot towel shaves.

We believe that going to the barber should be more than just a task. It should be an experience.

It's important to us that you get a great haircut, receive remarkable service and build lasting relationships in a comfortable space.

WHAT WE DO

HAIRCUT

Consultation, cut, wash, blow dry, and style with premium products. Includes a complimentary hot lather neck shave with straight razor.

BEARD TRIM

Consultation, trim, straight razor edging, and hot & cold towel combo. Finished with after-shave, lotion, and your choice of beard oil or balm.

HOT TOWEL SHAVE

Pre-shave lotion massage and hot towel steam for a closer, more comfortable shave. Followed by a cold towel press to close pores and cleanse face, finished with a cooling after-shave tonic.
